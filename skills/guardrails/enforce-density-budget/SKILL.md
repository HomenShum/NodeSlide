# Enforce Density Budget

1. Assign each slide and region a word, line, and minimum-font budget from its narrative job and geometry.
2. Preserve frozen claims, source IDs, and decision-critical qualifiers as protected content.
3. Measure estimated wrapped lines and rendered text capacity before export.
4. When over budget, issue a targeted repair: region, current words, target words, protected claims, and permitted visual substitution.
5. Prefer labels, annotations, charts, diagrams, and speaker notes over shrinking type.
6. Rerender and repeat within a bounded repair budget.

Do not solve density by deleting evidence, inventing abbreviations, shrinking below the legibility floor, or moving essential content off canvas.

Completion requires zero likely overflow, no internal overlap, preserved claims, and a full-size render inspection.
