# Require Visual Artifacts

1. Map every slide to narrative job, claim, evidence source, dominant artifact, and editability requirement.
2. Reject prose-only substitutes for a requested chart, diagram, screenshot, timeline, architecture, or comparison.
3. Require the artifact to carry decision-relevant information; decorative shapes and placeholder panels do not count.
4. Check that the artifact is large enough to inspect and that annotations point to visible evidence.
5. Validate the exported PPTX primitive and rendered slide.

For a seven-slide evidence deck, require at least five meaningful visual slides unless the frozen brief states otherwise.

Completion requires every requested artifact, no placeholder masquerading as proof, and preserved source lineage.
