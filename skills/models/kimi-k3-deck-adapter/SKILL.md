# Kimi K3 Deck Adapter

Treat this adapter as provisional until a controlled skill-on ablation passes.

## Contract

1. Verify the route is live; otherwise invoke `no-generic-fallback` and stop.
2. Bind every slide to its narrative job, required claims, evidence source, and dominant artifact before composition.
3. Generate three materially different composition plans for repeated or ambiguous slide jobs. At least one must change the dominant primitive.
4. Apply `require-visual-artifacts`; do not accept prose-only architecture, process, evidence, or comparison slides.
5. Apply `enforce-density-budget` before rendering.
6. Render the complete deck, inspect thumbnails and full-size slides, then apply `repair-internal-overlap` where needed.
7. Recheck frozen claims and unsupported claims after repair.

## Completion checks

- Live requested route is proven.
- Required-claim coverage passes.
- Every proof slide has an evidence-serving artifact.
- No likely text overflow or internal overlap remains.
- Direction pairs differ structurally, not only verbally.

Do not use Kimi as the sole final taste judge. Emit the failure class and evidence when any check remains open.
